Still have Doubts?

Here are some of the answers to common questions about our Services


How does dry cleaning differ from regular washing?

Dry cleaning uses specialized solvents instead of water to clean clothes, preserving fabric quality and preventing shrinkage or color fading.

Do you separate colors and fabrics before washing?

Yes, we separate clothes by color and fabric type to ensure optimal cleaning and to prevent color bleeding

Can you press delicate fabrics like silk or satin?

Yes, our team uses specialized techniques and equipment to safely press delicate fabrics without damaging them.

Can all stains be removed?

While we use advanced stain removal techniques, the success depends on the stain type and how long it's been set. We do our best to restore your garment.

Is it safe to dry clean leather and suede items?

Yes, we use specialized cleaning methods and products designed specifically for leather and suede to maintain their texture and color.

Do you offer wedding dress preservation after cleaning?

Yes, we provide preservation services to protect your wedding dress from yellowing, aging, and environmental damage.

Should I remove the hooks before sending curtains for cleaning?

No, you can leave the hooks on. We handle the cleaning process carefully without damaging any hardware.

Do you clean all types of rugs, including oriental and Persian rugs?

Yes, we specialize in cleaning all rug types, including delicate oriental and Persian rugs, using safe, effective cleaning methods.

Can you remove tough stains like ink or grease from uniforms?

Yes, we use specialized stain removal techniques suitable for tough stains commonly found on uniforms.

Is it safe to dry clean high-end designer garments?

Absolutely. We use gentle cleaning methods suitable for delicate fabrics and intricate designs, ensuring the preservation of quality and fit.

Do you offer in-home sofa cleaning services?

Yes, we provide in-home sofa cleaning for convenience and to ensure a thorough, professional clean without moving the furniture.

Is there an additional charge for pick-up and delivery?

Our pick-up and delivery service is free within certain areas. Please contact us to check availability and any applicable charges.

Do you offer discounts on bulk cleaning orders?

Yes, we provide special pricing for bulk cleaning, ideal for hotels, uniforms, and large households. Contact us for a custom quote.
